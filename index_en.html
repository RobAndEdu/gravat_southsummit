About us

Prefeitura Municipal de Gravataí.

  • 4th economy of Rio Grande do Sul;
  • One of the 100 largest in Brazil;
  • Gravataí plays a key role in the metropolitan region of Porto Alegre;
  • The city is recognized strong in the sectors of industry, commerce and services;
  • Highlight reflected in innovation;
  • Stimulation initiatives based on the regulatory framework;
  • The city is home to technological centers, incubators and laboratories.

Founded an emancipated on October 23, 1880.
The 5th most populous municipality in RS.
Area 68.3 Km² - Population 285,546 inhabitants (2021).
GDP BRL 11.964.051.909,00 (2018).
GPD per capita BRL 42.820,82 (2018).
investment fee 12% of RLC.
